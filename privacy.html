Privacy Policy – Inklu

Last updated: 2025-12-21

This Privacy Policy explains how the Inklu app (“the App”) handles information. The App is designed to work without collecting personal data.

1. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or process any personal data. The App does not require an account, and it does not ask for your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal information.

2. Tracking & Analytics

The App does not use tracking technologies and does not use analytics services that collect user data.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate third-party services that collect personal data (e.g., advertising SDKs).

4. Data Sharing

Because the App does not collect personal data, it does not share personal data with third parties.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App does not collect personal data from anyone, including children.

6. Changes to this Policy

If the App’s functionality changes in a way that affects privacy, this policy will be updated on this page.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the contact information shown on the App Store listing.
